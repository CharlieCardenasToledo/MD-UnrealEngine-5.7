# Nintendo Switch

> Source: https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/unreal-engine/development-for-nintendo-switch-in-unreal-engine

> Application Version: 5.7

Get started developing for Nintendo Switch with Unreal Engine.

Access to the Unreal Engine Nintendo Switch documentation is available without additional cost or licensing to any Unreal Engine developer who has met all NDA requirements and been granted the necessary permissions.

To gain access to Nintendo Switch documentation:

- If your company has already completed the console request process, ask your administrator to grant you Nintendo Switch documentation access from the [Epic Developer Portal](https://dev.epicgames.com/portal).
- If you or your company have not already begun the console request process then submit a **first time** or **additional access** request at [forms.unrealengine.com/s/form-console-access-request](https://forms.unrealengine.com/s/form-console-access-request) You will need to provide confirmation of you or your company’s approved Nintendo Switch developer status and may need to accept an Epic Games Console NDA. Epic staff will guide you through the process.
