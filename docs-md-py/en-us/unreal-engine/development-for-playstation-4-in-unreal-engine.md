# PlayStation 4

> Source: https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/unreal-engine/development-for-playstation-4-in-unreal-engine

> Application Version: 5.7

Get started developing for Playstation 4 with Unreal Engine.

Access to the Unreal Engine Playstation 4 documentation is available without additional cost or licensing to any Unreal Engine developer who has met all NDA requirements and been granted the necessary permissions.

To gain access to Playstation 4 documentation:

- If your company has already completed the console request process, ask your administrator to grant you Playstation 4 access from the Unreal Engine > Consoles page on the [Epic Developer Portal](https://dev.epicgames.com/portal).
- If your company or you have not already begun the console request process then submit a **first time** or **additional access** request at [forms.unrealengine.com/s/form-console-access-request](https://forms.unrealengine.com/s/form-console-access-request). You will need to provide confirmation of you or your company’s approved Playstation 4 developer status and may need to accept an Epic Games Console NDA. Epic staff will guide you through the process.
